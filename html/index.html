Debits and credits should sum to 0. We can verify this like so:

   1 select
   2 sum(direction * amount)
   3 from transactions;

Find transactions where debits don't match credits:

   1 select
   2   id,
   3   sum(direction * amount) as s
   4 from
   5   transactions
   6 group by
   7   id
   8 having
   9   s != 0;

Putting together a balance sheet is easy:

   1 select
   2   (account) as a,
   3   name,
   4   sum(amount * direction * normal) as balance
   5 from
   6   transactions
   7   left join accounts on a = accounts.number
   8 group by
   9   name
   10 order by
   11   a,
   12   name;

Here is a report with sub-accounts rolled into the main account:

   1 select
   2   ((account / 100) * 100) as a,
   3   name,
   4   sum(amount * direction * normal) as balance
   5 from
   6   transactions
   7   left join accounts on a = accounts.number
   8 group by
   9   name
   10 order by
   11   a,
   12   name;

Display all transactions in a human readable way:

   1  select
   2    id,
   3    date,
   4    name,
   5    case when direction = 1 then amount end as DR,
   6    case when direction = -1 then amount end as CR
   7  from
   8    transactions
   9    left join accounts on account = account.number
   10 order by
   11   id,
   12   date,
   13   CR,
   14   DR;
